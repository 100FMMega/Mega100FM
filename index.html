About Us

Mega 100 FM is a leading youth-focused radio station based in Gulu City, Northern Uganda. Broadcasting on 100.0 MHz, the station delivers a vibrant mix of music, entertainment, news, and community-focused programming that resonates with young people across the region. Since its establishment, Mega 100 FM has become a trusted Northern Uganda’s energetic and creative radio for youth. With a blend of urban hits, sports, and interactive talk shows, the station offers an authentic platform for expression, education, and entertainment. At Mega 100 FM, we believe in the power of radio to inform, inspire, and unite communities. We are not just a station — we are a movement driving the rhythm of Northern Uganda’s new generation. Frequency: 100.0 MHz Location: Olya Road, Gulu City, Uganda.
